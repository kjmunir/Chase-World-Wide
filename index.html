About Me

Meet Chase Munir-Allston

Chase is a 15-year-old sophomore at Mountain Island Charter—chill, low-drama, suburban, and discerning. He keeps a small circle, values peace, and lets his work on the court speak for itself. Basketball isn't just something he does; it's the lane he's chosen to pursue seriously, with genuine college athletic ambitions.

On the floor, Chase plays both power forward and small forward. He's an explosive rebounder, has a high defensive IQ, and is a strong finisher at the rim. He loves setting up teammates, doing the little things that don't always show up in the box score, and making winning plays.

Off the court, Chase is just as intentional. He's enrolled in the Gaston College dual enrollment program, putting him on track to graduate high school with an associate's degree in Cyber Security by age 17. That means he'll enter college academically ahead, with full athletic eligibility and less pressure than most incoming freshmen.

Why Support

Why Support Chase?

Chase plays basketball year-round—high school season, travel ball, training, camps, and leadership programs. That means constant investment: shoes, balls, uniforms, equipment, training sessions, gym memberships, camp fees, tournament fees, travel, food, and everything that comes with a growing teenager.

The 2026 summer travel league alone costs over $2,500, and that doesn’t include food, protein, haircuts, clothes, or the cost of showing up to college visits and camps he’s been invited to. Sponsorship isn’t just appreciated—it’s what makes it possible for him to say “yes” to these opportunities.

Your support helps Chase build something bigger than a season: a foundation of discipline, exposure, and experience that will follow him into college and beyond.

  • Camp fees, showcases, and leadership programs like FELC

  • College visits and on-campus camp invitations
